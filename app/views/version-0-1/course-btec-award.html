Course

BTEC Award for IT Users (Entry Level 3)

Course summary

Our Employability Programme is a two-week intensive course designed to support learners on their journey back to work. It addresses key barriers, gives an insight into current world of work, different ways of Job Searching and new approaches to the application process. Leads to an accredited BTEC Certificate.

Core modules include Rights and Responsibilities at Work, Working in a Team, Searching and Applying for Jobs, Preparing for Interview, Interview Skills and key next steps.
